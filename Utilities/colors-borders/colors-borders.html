Borders with colored text.

You can make use of the following classes to add borders, border-radius and colors to your text

Blue text dark-blue bd
Pink text dark-pink bd
Pink text dark-violet bd
brown text blackish bd
blue text pink-blue bd
grey text dark-violet bd 

          <div class="blue-1-bdr800 blue-color500 width-cw pd-1">

            Pink text dark-pink bd
