UTILITIES

- Utilities can be used to instanly assign values or colors to the html document.

How to use

If there is one hyphen between numbers then it's a decimal value example 2-5 is 2.5 if there is two hyphen (--) then it means it has two values

Margin and Padding Utilities

These can be usefull for quickly adding margins/padding to an element.

Example-

This line has a margin of 2.5rem and padding of 0.5rem. 


          <p class="mgn-2-5 pd-0-5 dark-1-bdr800 width-cw">
             This line has a margin of 2.5rem and padding of 0.5rem.
          </p>

Setting different values for top/bottom and left/right

This line has a margin of 2.5rem top/bottom and 1.5rem left/right and a padding of 4rem top/bottom and 0.5rem left/right. 


           <p class="mgn-2-5--1-5 pd-4--0-5 dark-1-bdr800 width-cw">
           This line has a margin of 2.5rem top/bottom and 1.5rem left/right
           and a padding of 4rem top/bottom and 0.5rem left/right.
          </p>

Single direction values

This line has a margin of 4rem bottom and a padding of 2rem left. 


          <p class="mgn-btm-4 pd-lt-2 dark-1-bdr800 width-cw">
          This line has a margin of 4rem bottom and a padding of 2rem left.
         </p>
